Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how we process personal data on this website in line with the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Purpose of processing

We process personal data solely to deliver our services, manage reservations and events, comply with legal obligations, and communicate with guests (for example via newsletters). We store data only for as long as necessary for these purposes or to meet statutory retention periods.

Categories of data

  • Master data (name, contact details)
  • Reservation details (date, time, party size, notes)
  • Communication data (emails, newsletter sign-ups)
  • Usage data (anonymous analytics to improve our offer)

Cookies and similar technologies

We use essential cookies that are required to run this website (for example to store your consent preferences or to secure forms). Optional cookies, such as analytics or marketing tools, are only activated with your consent. You can withdraw consent at any time via your browser settings.

Storing your cookie choices is based on Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR (legal obligation) and Art. 8 FADP. Essential cookies are necessary to operate the site reliably.

Use of service providers

To operate and automate our services we rely on trusted processors, such as Google (for managing event data) or email delivery partners. These providers process data strictly on our behalf and under contractual safeguards that ensure an adequate level of protection.

Your rights

You may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, and — where applicable — data portability. You can also object to processing and withdraw consent with future effect. Please send related enquiries to hello@pinte.amatt.ch.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy to reflect new legal requirements or additional services. The version published on this page applies in each case.
